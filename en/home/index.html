ABOUT ME

I’m an architect by training with almost 10 years of experience in the industrial sector. However, some time ago I decided to take a new direction and pursue what I’m truly passionate about: programming. Right now, I’m studying Multiplatform Application Development (DAM) to fully prepare myself and take my first steps into the software world. Born and raised in Barcelona, I see myself as a curious and persistent person, always eager to learn and take on new challenges. I also have two greyhounds who keep me motivated and provide great company during this new chapter. I love combining the creativity I developed in architecture with the logic and structure that programming requires. I believe this mix gives me a unique perspective to solve problems and build practical solutions. I’m really excited about this career change and everything that’s ahead.
